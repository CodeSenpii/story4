Looking towards the east one early morningmorning I saw a glow over the horizon. It was still dark outsid and the twinkling stars glittered delightfully over the world. The emerging glow looked like something was set ablaze in the distance. I could not see spark flames or smoke just a small but very noticeable that interrupted the darkness in the distance. The far light was made up of many colors red, orange yellow and a tinge of blue where it met the sky. light stretches a great distance across the expanse finally fading back into darkness at the furthest points The most intense light came from the center of the blaze which was straight ahead me. As I gazed upon this phenomenon minute by minute it grew brighter and brighter, reaching up even further into the sky Replacing the darkness and outing the stars close to its edges. Then I saw it, peeping over the curved surface of the earth like a curious spectator peering over a fence; It was the source of the great light. The were all gone at this point; hidden away behind the morning sky. All the creature of the air began to sing almost in unison at the coming of the new day. As the minutes tick by the curious spectator swelled to round, bright ball of light. No longer being able directly at the fierce circle of light I turned my back towards it. I could feel a gentle warming effect evading my body starting from the back of my neck spreading to my shoulders and down my back. The warmth comforted me and made me feel alive. What is this creature? is it a dragon? Is it a great volcano erupting behind me? What can be so bright that it share its light and heat with the entire world? What can give life to a wilted flower? What can so easily usurp the night and make the stars flee behind the deep blue sky? I turned again to face the silent usurper with my hand over my eyes. A warm sensation filled my face. Sliding my hand slowly away I opened my eye and I saw it, only for a moment as my vision was overwhelmed by its radiance.
